Xin Jin

Research Scientist at Meta GenAI, Ph.D.

Hi! I’m a Research Scientist at Meta GenAI, working on coding copilots. I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University in 2024, advised by Prof. Zhiqiang Lin. I was a research intern at Microsoft Research and Cisco Research during summer 2023 and summer 2022.

I am broadly interested in large language models (LLMs), program analysis, and security. I am particularly interested in:

  • Program Comprehension and Generation: Understanding program semantics with LLMs/machine learning and generating program code in various programming languages.
  • Software Security: Securing software systems by program analysis and machine learning.
  • Threat Intelligence: Collecting, interpreting, and enriching cybersecurity data to identify and mitigate potential threats and assist decision-making processes.

I am always looking for opportunities to collaborate. The best way to reach me is via email: jin (dot) 967 (at) osu (dot) edu.
