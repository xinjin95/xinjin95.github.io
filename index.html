Xin Jin

Research Scientist at Meta, Ph.D.

Hi! I’m a research scientist at the Llama Research team, Meta GenAI. I focus on advancing the coding capabilities of next-generation Llama models.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University in 2024, advised by Prof. Zhiqiang Lin. My research interests broadly lie in large language models (LLMs), program analysis, and security.

I am always looking for opportunities to collaborate. The best way to reach me is via email: jin (dot) 967 (at) osu (dot) edu.
